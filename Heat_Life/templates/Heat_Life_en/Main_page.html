About production

The production process for heatlife films is complex and includes several technological steps that are carefully controlled by plant personnel. The main production line is fully automated, with personnel supervising production. The main components of "heatlife" films are two heating carbon layers, which gradually impregnate the non-woven material. After the first layer has dried, a second layer of carbon is applied to ensure better quality and durability of the films. Protective layers are also applied, making the “heatlife” film resistant to mechanical damage. For the film "heatlife" hl-220/m2 (with grounding), the manufacturing process is more complicated, since it is necessary to apply a special layer to relieve static voltage and glue in an additional copper grounding bus.
